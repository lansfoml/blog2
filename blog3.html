Flexbox specifically gives you control over your direction, distribution, alignment, and expanison. The first element we are going ot talk about is direction. When you decide top use flexbox is tarts off with the direction in row. You can change it to be a column by doing: flex-direction: column; as shown below.

The next thing flexbox allows you to do is manage the sapcing between the items in your flexbox. This is done by the justify-content element. There are many different things you can do with justify-content including start, center, space-between, space-around, sapce-evenly and more. Personally I like to use the cetner and sapce-evenly elements as they can cetner the content or space the items evenly for a clean look. Another way to manage the spacing and layout of items in your flexbox would be using the align-items element.
